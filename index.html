Uganda

Uganda, often referred to as the "Pearl of Africa," is a country rich in natural beauty and cultural diversity. It's home to the endangered Mountain Gorillas in Bwindi Impenetrable Forest, offering visitors a unique opportunity for gorilla trekking. Uganda also boasts stunning landscapes, from the majestic Rwenzori Mountains to the vast Lake Victoria. With its friendly people, vibrant culture, and incredible wildlife, including the famous Source of the Nile in Jinja, Uganda is a hidden gem for travelers seeking adventure and unforgettable experiences.
